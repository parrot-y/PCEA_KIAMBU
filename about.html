Leadership Message

Dear Students, Parents, and Esteemed Members of Our School Community, It is with great pride and pleasure that I welcome you to our school. Whether you are joining us for the first time or continuing your journey with us, you are a valued part of a community committed to learning, growth, and excellence.

Our school is more than a place of academic instruction it is a place where character is built, talents are nurtured, and lifelong values are shaped. We strive to create a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment where every student is encouraged to reach their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally.

Education is a shared responsibility. The strong partnership between our dedicated staff, supportive families, and motivated students is the foundation of our success. Together, we work to inspire curiosity, foster respect, and prepare our learners to become responsible, confident, and compassionate citizens. As Principal, I am honored to lead this school and remain committed to upholding high standards while ensuring that every child feels seen, heard, and supported. I look forward to another year of collaboration, achievement, and growth.

Our Academic Philosophy

At PCEA KIAMBU ACADEMY AND JUNIOR SCHOOL, we move beyond rote memorization. Our approach is rooted in the Competency Based Education (CBC), focusing on what the learner can actually do rather than just what they know.

Learner-Centered Approach

We believe every child is unique. Our small class sizes allow teachers to provide personalized attention, ensuring no student is left behind.

Holistic Development

Education at PCEA KIAMBU ACADEMY AND JUNIOR SCHOOL extends beyond the four walls of a classroom. From the music room to the club activities, we provide diverse platforms for talent discovery.

Technology in Education

We integrate modern tools and multimedia devices into our daily lessons, preparing our giants for a digitally-driven future.
